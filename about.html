About

Most marketing exists just to exist. We started The First Three because we believe attention is only worth capturing if you do something meaningful with it.

Those first three seconds where someone decides to stop or keep scrolling are more than a metric. They're an opportunity to connect and say something that actually matters.

At The First Three, everything starts with intention.

Blending strategy, storytelling, and visually-driven content, we create work that feels considered, culturally aware, and built to perform.

We work with brands who aren't just chasing views. They want their marketing to mean something to their audience, to their industry, and to the world they're operating in. We help them show up with purpose.

In a world of endless content, only what's intentional stands out.
Less noise. More meaning.
